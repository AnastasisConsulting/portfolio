Founder's Story

William Wallace Morehead

"What do you intend to do with your time"

The question came at a fundraiser. I wasn't a computer scientist; I had just earned an Associate's in Welding Technology. But the answer that came out surprised even me:
"I want to build AI tools for future generations to mitigate my generation's deferred responsibilities."

I began researching global systemic crises, and as a direct result, I blueprinted 'City SeCUReD'. However, when investigating current technology, I found irreconcilable failures in both LLMs and persistence systems.

This is when the Pre-Linguistic Inference Engine was born.

Instead of giving LLMs free reign to manufacture tokens, I created a Symbolic Logic Layer to bootstrap them, channeling the collapse of gradient descent into a logical symbolic processor—making hallucination virtually impossible.
