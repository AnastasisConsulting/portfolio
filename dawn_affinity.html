THE THREE-GEAR CHURN

SUBJECT: Politico-Economic Interaction & Fractal State Propagation

Triadic Logic System

The world state of Eideus is not static; it is driven by a hierarchical "Three-Gear Churn" model that simulates the friction between three core societal drivers: **Politics**, **Diplomacy**, and **Economy**.

// AFFINITY MODIFIERS

  • STRENGTH (STR): Increases Political Power, Decreases Diplomacy (Force-first).
  • INTELLIGENCE (INT): Boosts Economy and Treaty States (Systemic stability).
  • DEXTERITY (DEX): High Economic Mobility, Negligible Political Weight (Agility/Underground).

M.O.S.S. Framework

The **M.otis O.perandi S.tyle S.ystem** handles the emergent behavior of NPCs. When a "Gear Shift" occurs in the simulation state, M.O.S.S. re-evaluates NPC stances, adjusting their narrative instructions (prompts) to reflect new power dynamics in the System.
