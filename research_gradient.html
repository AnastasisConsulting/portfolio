Building a 4D Gradient

SUBJECT: Temporal Depth in Recursive Semantic State-Machines

CORE CONCEPT

Traditional semantic lattices utilize 3D coordinates (X, Y, Z) to map meaning. This research introduces a fourth dimension—**Temporal Gradient (Time-T)**—which tracks the evolution of a data node across multiple narrative or logical cycles.

THE 4D VECTOR

V(x, y, z) + T(evolution_index)

By adding T, we can differentiate between "Context as it exists" and "Context as it was modified by the recent turn engine".
