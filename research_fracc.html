F.R.A.C.C. Cognitive Architecture Research

SUBJECT: Fractal Recursive Adaptive Cognitive Chains

ABSTRACT

This paper introduces F.R.A.C.C. (Fractal Recursive Adaptive Cognitive Chains), a novel cognitive architecture designed to enable recursive self-improvement and adaptive reasoning in AI systems. By decomposing complex problems into fractal sub-structures, the system achieves hierarchical reasoning across multiple abstraction layers.

"The F.R.A.C.C. architecture represents a paradigm shift from linear inference chains to recursive, self-modifying cognitive structures that mirror the fractal nature of human thought processes."

  • // C2: Adaptive chain modification based on intermediate results
  • // C3: Fractal pattern recognition across cognitive hierarchies
