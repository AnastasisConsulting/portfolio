The Universal Output Mechanism

THE FRACTAL TEMPLATE SYSTEM

We confront the AI hallucination and accountability problem with a tailored compiler rendering outputs rather than generating them.

Starting from Paul Ekman's "Truth Wizards" and micro-expressions research, we surmised that if universal outputs exist regardless of culture or language, a **universal BIOS** must too exist at the pre-linguistic level.

When we asked "Why 7" this led to **Miller's Law** (7 ± 2). Adding the **Triadic Law** for causality as first principles for temporal coherence and the universe's prime example of recursion (the fractal), we had our engine.

OUR PROCESS IS:

  • Domain-Agnostic: 7-Arc Decomposition collapses any input into universal rhetorical primitives (Essence to Value), bypassing domain-specific bloat.
  • Model Agnostic: Logic resides in the deterministic Triadic Evaluator; LLMs are utilized solely as interchangeable rendering engines.
  • Linguistically Agnostic: Spatial coordinate mapping `(t, x, y, z)` anchors intent in a non-verbal 3D lattice, independent of syntax or language.
  • Universally Applicable: The Risk/Reward/Relation triadic phases provide a fundamental causality model for gaming, enterprise, or crisis management.
  • Easy to Understand: Spatial visualization of AI state through the 7x7x7 Voxel Grid makes the "Black Box" of inference human-parsable.
  • Highly Organized: Deterministic metadata tagging across 6-face nodes ensuring every memory has a persistent, addressable index.
  • Adaptable to Any Use Case: Modular integration via hooks like `useMemoryCore` allows deployment across standard web, local Python, or OS-level stacks.
  • Affordable: Hybrid edge/cloud topology resolves context locally via Cosine Similarity on Coral TPUs, cutting token overhead by ~80%.

The Problem Space

A Shared Challenge, Not a Silver Bullet

The AI industry loses an estimated $3 billion annually to three compounding failures.

We don't claim to have solved them. We believe we've found a structural approach worth investigating.

HALLUCINATION

LLMs generate plausible but fabricated outputs. Our approach forces token collapse onto pre-defined structural scaffolds, constraining generation to render within verified frameworks rather than inventing freely.

SEMANTIC DRIFT

Over extended interactions, models lose coherence. The 343-node holographic lattice provides spatially-indexed persistent memory, anchoring context across sessions rather than relying on linear token windows.

ACCOUNTABILITY

Probabilistic outputs resist auditability. By decomposing domains into addressable fractal coordinates, every output maps to a traceable node — making the reasoning path inspectable, not opaque.

These are hard, open problems. Our contribution is a structural hypothesis — tested across 12 applied systems — that deterministic scaffolding can coexist with generative flexibility. We welcome scrutiny.
