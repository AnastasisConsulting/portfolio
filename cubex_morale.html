MORALE SYSTEMS

SUBJECT: Emergent Team Dynamics & Relation Metrics

Structural Morale

In CubeX³, pieces are not merely material values—they are agents within a **Morale Lattice**. The `MoraleSystem` calculates team coherence based on survival time, kill-ratios, and proximity to the 'Crown' (King).

TEAM COHERENCE INDEX

// THE "RELATION" SCALAR

Part of the RRR Evaluation. It measures how much the loss of a specific piece impacts the 'will' of the remaining units.

// RESCUE OPERATIONS

A unique promotion mechanic where piece-promotion can be sacrificed to 'rescue' a fallen comrade, restoring massive Morale.

// GENESIS EVOLUTION

This early social logic served as the foundation for the **Affinity Gears** in Eideus Dawn, where AI agents develop long-term emotional and mechanical bonds based on shared history.
